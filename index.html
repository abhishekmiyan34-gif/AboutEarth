Few facts about the world we live in

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only astronomical object known to harbor life.
The Earth is the densest planet in the Solar System and the largest of the four terrestrial planets.
The Earth was formed approximately 4.54 billion years ago, and life appeared on its surface within its first billion years.
It is the only planet known to support life and has a diverse range of ecosystems, climates, and geological features.

Top 3 facts about the Earth

  1. Earth is the only planet in our solar system that has liquid water on its surface.
  2. Earth's atmosphere is composed of 78% nitrogen, 21% oxygen, and trace amounts of other gases.
  3. Earth has a magnetic field that protects it from harmful solar radiation.
